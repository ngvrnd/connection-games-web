Connection Games Support

Effective date: April 18, 2026

If you need help with Connection Games, found a bug, or want to suggest a feature, please get in touch by email.

Support contact

Email: ngvrnd@gmail.com

Best information to include

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS or iPadOS version
  • The app version or TestFlight build number, if you know it
  • Which game you were playing: Hex, Atoll, or Y
  • Whether the issue happened in local play or online play
  • Whether the app came from the App Store, TestFlight, or a direct Xcode install
  • A short description of what happened
  • Clear steps to reproduce the issue, if you can reproduce it
  • A screenshot, if one helps explain the problem

Online play issues

  • Which device had the issue
  • Whether the issue involved invites, matchmaking, chat, reminders, or turn sync
  • Whether both players were on the same app build

Connection Games is an independent game project, so support is provided by email.
