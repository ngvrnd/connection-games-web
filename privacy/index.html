Connection Games Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

Connection Games is designed to collect as little information as possible.

Information the app handles

  • Local game settings and game state may be stored on your device so the app can remember your preferences and continue gameplay.
  • If you use online play features that rely on Apple Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center account information, player identity, and match-related data to provide those features.

Advertising and tracking

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are used.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used.
  • The app is not designed to track you across apps or websites.

Data sharing

  • The developer does not sell personal information.
  • The developer does not share personal information with third-party advertisers.
  • Online multiplayer features rely on Apple services such as Game Center, which are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to use online play features.
  • You can manage Game Center settings through your Apple account and device settings.
  • You can delete the app from your device at any time.
