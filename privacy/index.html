Connection Games Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Connection Games is designed to collect as little information as possible.

Information the app handles

  • Local game settings and local game state may be stored on your device so the app can remember preferences and continue gameplay.
  • If you use online play features that rely on Apple Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center account information, player identity, match invitations, turn data, reminder notifications, and other match-related data needed to provide those features.
  • If you use in-match chat during online play, chat messages are transmitted using Apple Game Center services as part of match activity.
  • If you contact support by email, the information you include in that message will be used to respond to your request.

Advertising and tracking

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are used.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used.
  • The app is not designed to track you across apps or websites.

Data sharing

  • The developer does not sell personal information.
  • The developer does not share personal information with third-party advertisers.
  • Online multiplayer features rely on Apple services such as Game Center, which are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to use online play features.
  • You can manage Game Center settings through your Apple account and device settings.
  • You can stop using in-match chat by avoiding online matches or discontinuing chat participation.
  • You can delete the app from your device at any time.
